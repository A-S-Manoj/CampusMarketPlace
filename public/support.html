ABOUT US

Bridging the Gap on Campus

CampusMarketplace is a student-driven platform designed to simplify the way we buy, sell, and lend items within our university community. Whether it's textbooks for the new semester, electronics, or dorm essentials, we provide a secure and efficient environment for campus trade.

Our mission is to foster sustainability and community by encouraging the reuse of quality items, helping students save money and reduce waste.

SUPPORT

Help Center

How do I list an item?

Simply navigate to the "Sell/Lend" page, fill in the product details, upload an image, and your listing will be live instantly for other students to see.

Is the payment handled through the site?

No, CampusMarketplace provides the platform for discovery and communication. Payments and item exchanges are handled privately between students on campus.

What if I encounter a problem?

You can report issues or suspicious listings through the "Contact Us" section above. Our team investigates all reports within 24 hours.
